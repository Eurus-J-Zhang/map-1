Welcome to the game introduction! The actual game interface will look just like this, with all these red notes hidden. Please use this page to familiarize yourself with the game and plan your route in advance, making it easier when the timer is running in the actual game.

Your task is to navigate the metro lines from Giles Town to Condy Vale. You will depart from Giles Town at 8:30, and must reach Condy Vale before 9:00 for an important meeting. Please calculate your time carefully and avoid unnecessary detours to ensure you arrive in time.

At each station, you will have 90 seconds to make a decision. After selecting your choice and clicking "Continue", you will be led to the next station. The clock time will increase according to your selection, but the 90-seconds decision time will not affect the clock.

There might be situations happening in the process, stay alert, it is part of the game.

How to win this game?
   Arrive at Condy Vale before 9:00.

How might you lose this game?
   Failing to arrive at Condy Vale by 9:00.
   Taking more than 90 seconds to make a decision at any station.
